Privacy Policy

TealApps will not collect any data from you.

The Pen & Paper app requests access to your storage area in order to store your pages there.
The pages will only be stored on your own device, unless you choose to use the 'share' button yourself to eg share with friends by email.
The pages and last page number will be stored on your device under TealApps/Paper.
