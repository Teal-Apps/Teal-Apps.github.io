Privacy Policy

TealApps will not collect any data from you.

The Pen & Paper app requests access to your storage area in order to store your pages there.
The pages will only be stored on your own device, unless you choose to use the 'share' button yourself to eg share with friends by email.
The pages will be stored on your device under TealApps/Paper or Android/data/com.tealapps.paper/files.
The Lottery app only stores your setting on your device.

Note that Google might collect data from the ads, eg approx. location, app interaction, crash logs, diagnostics, device id.
